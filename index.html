Medical Device Success

The difference between a promising device and a successful device is whether it's adopted in real-world clinical practice.

To gain adoption, a device must solve for both the stakeholders who decide its fate—regulators, adopters, administrators, and payers—and the evidence they require: safety, efficacy, value, and outcomes.

Fortunately, complexity is where opportunity lies.

With a unique blend of commercial skills and technical experience, Dabrowiak Consulting can help bring the puzzle pieces together.

Services

Product Strategy & Support

Dabrowiak Consulting systematically gathers stakeholder input and translates it into optimal design priorities, development opportunities, and commercialization plans.

This maximizes alignment with stakeholder needs, resulting in greater productivity, smoother launches, and faster adoption.

Independent Technical Assessment

Dabrowiak Consulting provides unbiased evaluation of technologies and claims to support strategic, intellectual property, or investment decisions.

Technical clarity combined with market sensibility delivers the confidence needed to act decisively in high-stakes contexts.

About

Jeremy Dabrowiak most recently served as Director of Marketing at ZOLL Medical. Prior to that, he served the company in Director-level roles in Innovation and Research, Design Research, and Product Engineering.

In 21 years of startup and corporate medical device roles, he has driven multiple products from concept to commercial launch while co-authoring 38 patents. Prior to medical devices, he worked for 5 years in biotechnology instrumentation, contributing to early work in Next-Generation Sequencing.

Jeremy has completed executive courses in innovation, continuous improvement, and engineering management at MIT Sloan and the University of Michigan. He holds degrees in Mechanical Engineering from Michigan Technological University (B.S. Summa Cum Laude) and Stanford University (M.S.).

His 1999 Master's degree project was a Parallel Parking Assistive System for Toyota.
