Medical Device Success

In MedTech, the difference between a promising device and a successful one is whether it's adopted in real-world clinical practice. Achieving adoption isn’t about solving one challenge in isolation. It’s about fitting together interconnected pieces—each with its own demands and priorities.

Every device must solve for both the stakeholders who decide its fate—regulators, adopters, payers, and administrators—and the evidence they require: safety, efficacy, value, and outcomes.

This complexity is where opportunity lies. Successful MedDev adoption requires cleverly solving for an array of technical and customer limitations.

With a unique blend of commercial and technical skills, Dabrowiak Consulting can help bring the pieces together.

About

Jeremy Dabrowiak most recently served as Director of Marketing at ZOLL Medical, following Director-level roles in Innovation and Research, Design Research, and Product Engineering.

In 21 years of startup and corporate medical device roles, he has driven multiple products from concept to commercial launch while co-authoring 38 patents. Prior to medical devices, he worked for 5 years in biotechnology instrumentation, contributing to early work in Next-Generation Sequencing.

Jeremy has completed executive courses in innovation, continuous improvement, and engineering management at MIT Sloan and the University of Michigan. He holds degrees in Mechanical Engineering from Michigan Technological University (B.S. Summa Cum Laude) and Stanford University (M.S.).

Services

Product Optimization

Customer-driven product support to minimize development effort while maximizing stakeholder satisfaction.

  • Development support including real-time VOC, product definition, and roadmapping
  • Customer research programs delivering actionable insights on clinical processes and preferences
  • Technical concept ideation and implementation guidance
  • Marketing support including positioning input, collateral, product aids, and training

Through structured analysis and disciplined VOC methods, Dabrowiak Consulting helps teams avoid wasted cycles and focus on what truly matters to customers. The result is smooth, confident product launches which maximize adoption and competitive advantage.

Technical Analysis

Independent opinion and clear communication to support IP development, strategy, or litigation.

  • Leveraging technical expertise across a variety of medical capital equipment, catheters, and other consumables
  • Technical patent claim reviews and prior art comparisons
  • Expert reports that explain complex technology in accessible terms
  • Insightful, accessible visuals to support courtroom or boardroom presentations

Through disciplined analysis and clear presentation, Dabrowiak Consulting strengthens legal teams with the technical clarity they need to argue persuasively. The goal is not just to dissect the technology, but to make its implications unmissable.

Due Diligence

Evidence-based evaluations of technologies and products to support investments or acquisition.

  • Evaluation of core technical feasibility, performance, and limitations
  • Assessment of usability and adoption likelihood based on customer needs and workflows
  • Identification of development risks, regulatory considerations, and potential failure modes
  • Translation of technical findings into clear implications for investors and deal teams

Dabrowiak Consulting combines deep technical intuition and experience with a customer and market perspective, offering clear and unique insights into whether a technology can deliver on its claims and gain real-world adoption.
